Communication techniques to improve your interactions

Trick 1:"Foot-in-the-Mouth" Technique ‚Äì "Poochne ka Asar"
Agar aap kisi se unka haal-chal ya mood poochte hain, aur wo positive jawab dete hain, toh aap unse koi chhoti si madad maang sakte hain. Isse wo aapko zyada pasand karte hain.
üß† Reason:
Jab log apni feelings ya mood share karte hain, toh wo aapke saath emotionally connect karte hain, aur unhe lagta hai ki aap unki care kar rahe ho, isliye wo madad karne ke liye tayar hote hain.
Trick 2:"Touch ka Power" ‚Äì "Chhoti Si Connection"
Agar aap kisi se baat karte waqt unko halki si touch de dete ho, jaise unke kandhe ya haath par, toh wo aapko zyada apna mehsoos karte hain.
üß† Reason:
Touch se brain ke bonding areas activate hote hain, jo ki logon ko comfortable aur connected feel karwate hain.
Trick 3:Scarcity Principle ‚Äì "Jitna Kam, Utna Zyada Keemti"
Agar koi cheez limited hai ya scarce hai, toh uska value zyada hota hai. Agar aap kuch exclusive ya limited time offer dikhate ho, toh log usse zyada value karte hain.
üß† Reason:
Jab kisi cheez ka scarcity hota hai, toh log usse jaldi apna lene ka sochte hain kyunki unhe lagta hai ki wo cheez miss na ho jaye (FOMO ‚Äì Fear of Missing Out).
Trick 4:"Halo Effect" ‚Äì "Ek Achha Trait, Sabko Dikhata Hai"
Agar kisi ne ek cheez achhe se ki hai, toh log usse assume karte hain ki wo dusri cheezon mein bhi achha hoga.
üß† Reason:
Hum apne judgment ko simplify karte hain, aur jab koi achha kaam karta hai, toh hum unhe baaki cheezon mein bhi positive manner mein dekhte hain.
Trick 5:Social Proof ‚Äì "Sab Log Kar Rahe Hain"
Jab aap dekhte ho ki dusre log kisi cheez ko kar rahe hain, toh aap bhi ussi cheez ko follow karne lagte ho. Jaise ki reviews, testimonials, ya koi crowded place.
üß† Reason:
Hum group ke saath chalna pasand karte hain kyunki isse humein lagta hai ki hum sahi kar rahe hain, aur humare decisions validated feel karte hain.
Trick 6:Reciprocity Norm ‚Äì "Aap Mere Liye, Main Aap Ke Liye"
Agar aap kisi ke liye kuch karte ho, chhota sa bhi, toh wo aapke liye kuch karne ki sochne lagte hain.
üß† Reason:
Insaan ki fitrat hoti hai ki wo kisi favor ka jawab favor se deta hai, aur yeh relationships ko mazboot banata hai.
Trick 7:Framing Effect ‚Äì "Perspective ka Power"
Agar kisi cheez ko ya question ko alag tareeke se present kiya jata hai, toh wo aapke decision ko influence kar sakta hai, chahe asli information same ho.
üß† Reason:
Hum cheezon ko kaise dekhein, yeh unki presentation pe depend karta hai. Agar cheez ko achha ya bura frame kiya jaye, toh humare decisions us hisaab se badalte hain.
Trick 8:Contrast Principle ‚Äì "Jab Kuch Compare Karna Ho"
Agar aap pehle koi unattractive option dikhate ho aur phir attractive option dikhate ho, toh doosra option zyada appealing lagta hai.
üß† Reason:
Hum apni judgment ko relative terms mein karte hain. Agar pehle wali cheez itni achi nahi thi, toh doosri cheez comparatively better lagti hai.
Trick 9:False Consensus Effect ‚Äì "Sab Mere Jaise Hi Hai"
Hum sochne lagte hain ki doosre log bhi humare opinions aur beliefs share karte hain, jabki wo zaroori nahi hota.
üß† Reason:
Yeh humare liye comfort ka kaam karta hai, kyunki hum sochne lagte hain ki hum galat nahi hain aur humare ideas ko support mil raha hai.
Trick 10:Authority Principle ‚Äì "Experts Par Bharosa"
Log un logo ki baat zyada sunte hain jo unhe expert ya authority figure lagte hain, chahe unka knowledge limited ho.
üß† Reason:
Hum authority figures ko trust karte hain, kyunki unki expertise se hum apne decisions ko easy bana lete hain aur uncertainty kam ho jati hai.
